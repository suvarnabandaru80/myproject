The Beginning

From a wardrobe
obsession to a movement

It started with a frustration. Founder Arjun Mehta had spent years buying into fast fashion, only to find his wardrobe full of clothes that felt hollow — nothing he truly loved or trusted. In 2019, he launched NOIR from a 200 sq/ft studio in Mumbai, sourcing the first twelve pieces himself from artisans in Portugal, Italy, and Kashmir.

The first drop sold out in 48 hours. Not because of marketing — because people could feel the difference. Every stitch had a reason. Every piece had a story. That was the moment NOIR stopped being a project and became a purpose.

What We Stand For

Our Values

01
Radical Transparency
Every product page lists the exact materials, origin country, and artisan behind the piece. We believe knowing the source is part of the luxury.
02
Slow Fashion Philosophy
We release two collections a year. No overproduction, no waste. We'd rather sell out than overproduce — it's better for the planet and better for the craft.
03
Fair Supply Chain
Every artisan we work with is paid above-market rates with verified fair trade certification. Luxury should uplift everyone in its chain, not just the end consumer.
04
Built to Last
We design for decades, not seasons. Our pieces come with a lifetime repair guarantee — if it breaks, we fix it. Wear it until it becomes part of your story.
05
Carbon Neutral by 2026
We offset 110% of our carbon footprint through reforestation projects in the Western Ghats and are on track for full carbon neutrality by Q4 2026.
06
Inclusive by Design
All NOIR pieces are designed and tested across a full size range. Style has no size limit — and neither should access to quality fashion.
